# Creating a custom world

To start, go to the landing page and select one of the three starter projects:

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FhAGR1f7c8eJvG7GpRuA7%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c1f43f3b&sv=2)

[Somnia City](https://mmleditor.com/projects/romantic-lime-giraffe-GVdzkA) \- Start from the Somnia City. Customize and create your own cityscape. [Somnia Village](https://mmleditor.com/projects/nervous-azure-reptile-LNcuUY) \- Start from the Somnia village. Customize and create your own medieval space. [Blank Space](https://mmleditor.com/projects/basic-silver-barracuda-8Umidy) \- Start from a blank space and build anything you can imagine! In this example we will start from the Somnia city project.

### 

Defining your world

Open the city project in the MML editor and click fork

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FYIVwROLp1XynLpiFep3K%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b1e6393f&sv=2)

This will create a version of the City space you can customize. For this tutorial, we are going to place a few preset objects in the space. With the World Builder, you can upload your own assets and add code to create interactive experiences!

Go to the assets tab in the world builder.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FabVePfKBzfjf0CvGxS38%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=910653c8&sv=2)

Here you will see all the premade assets you can add to the scene. I'm going to add a few palm trees to make the city feel a bit more tropical. To do this click and drag the palmtree.glb into the edit window.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FbExutvevju2L0yb3C9rX%2FScreenshot%25202024-09-27%2520at%252011.04.49.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=dab3dfa9&sv=2)

Location Gizmo

Intially the tree will probably be in a random place. Move it using the location gizmo, which allows you to move it up, down, left, right, forward and back. You click hold and drag to do this.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FZZow5gLbpgXhMkrtZQAo%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=684dc285&sv=2)

I've now moved the tree into a better position. You will also notice the MML code below has updated to reflect my changes. You can move the tree around by directly updating the code as well. I am now going to go back to the scene view and make a few copies of the tree.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2F2HlhRHkV4BNNO5VBoUMr%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=2ba0d05b&sv=2)

To do this, I will copy and paste the m-model code that references the tree (the second line). This will put three trees in the scene. However, currently, they are all on top of each other!

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FseUc7RUSY5Dhn9c4x3Tm%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b2ec4b02&sv=2)

I will now select the other two trees and move them around to make a little tropical forest.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FUDZCRC3IBZM0ftU6EpwR%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=3a508f54&sv=2)

Remember you can move the camera around in the editor window. More detail on this can be found in the [mml editor tour](/playground-documentation/beginner-guides/the-mml-editor-tour). Ok now I've got a cool looking space I'm going to mint it into an NFT and load it into the playground! First copy the mml code of your world. Then go to the world builder app.

### 

Minting your world

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FCn5djiYzmHMtpMzvHYWB%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=5b1a391a&sv=2)

Copy the mml code from the editor into the world definition box:

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FlI8vbNG5Rddhj6syLmBZ%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=1da440fb&sv=2)

Go through the next step and take a picture of your world:

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FDpfI8INUe1oUP6UnQBw6%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=8eb513ad&sv=2)

Give your world a name and a description.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FurYmRehs2alVgRrhSTxM%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b27d638c&sv=2)

Review everything and mint your world. If anything is wrong you can hit back to repeat previous steps.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FgPWTtc5EmrHA5ojtIKo4%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=46fd7f55&sv=2)

You will need to confirm the transaction to mint the NFT in your wallet:

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FILoyGA4I5QtvNdMH9h2i%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=4e938444&sv=2)

Once minted you can go to the playground and load your world!

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FlHqAE22HxejN9Atv33Zp%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=1695cd17&sv=2)

Click the button and you will be loaded in the Playground.

### 

Playing in your world

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FjYUqHT2EAqvuVbbU9y0B%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bd9ade87&sv=2)

Here click the load world button. You will then be presented with a prompt:

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2F8UNobZQfSRUYhYaHMhkC%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=dccb4d8&sv=2)

Click the load world button and you will be able to select your custom world from the list!

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2F66NvJnodkaqw2Ocg5W8g%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bce0f0c2&sv=2)

In this list find your world and click the view world button (the little eye) 

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FZfsSMFySkLonyap9Gdob%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=44e2b163&sv=2)

Finally click the **create world** button. This will create a server with your world. You can now click jump in to play or share with the invite url.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FQgixflydDAfPsfcE4tOG%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=39c0ddcf&sv=2)

Congratz you are now in your world!

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FvM4GB1ipjuXoB7dlFl3w%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=d2e8e7ca&sv=2)

### 

Help I'm stuck below the map!

Now you may spawn below the ground of your map. Don't worry this is easy to fix. Go back to your mml project with your world.

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FZLljWcec0vZtLmWWUDIv%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c8214830&sv=2)

Set the y value of all your object to 0. Currently mine look like this: 

Copy
    
    
    <m-model id="environment" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/avatar-v2-assets/environments/city-compresed.glb" x
    x="-0.118" y="3.673" z="-0.129">
    </m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-6.474" y="3.636" z="-1.908"></m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-6.474" y="3.636"></m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-8.777" y="3.636"></m-model>

I will now set all the y value of the objects to 0. This will make my mml look like this:

Copy
    
    
    <m-model id="environment" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/avatar-v2-assets/environments/city-compresed.glb" 
    x="-0.118" y="0.0" z="-0.129"></m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-6.474" y="0.0" z="-1.908"></m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-6.474" y="0.0"></m-model>
    <m-model src="https://mmlstorage.com/0ecc6c086be8685d6fb5d89556096d5b26a569000b667957aa31c633dacc5a12" 
    x="-8.777" y="0.0"></m-model>

You should now see your world slightly down in the scene: 

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2FJKj58SfjjI1zLA502sRu%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=d3ac5dac&sv=2)

Go and rebuild your world by following the same steps in the [minting your world section](/playground-documentation/getting-started/creating-a-custom-world#minting-your-world) and [playing in your world](/playground-documentation/getting-started/creating-a-custom-world#playing-in-your-world).

![](https://docs.somnia.network/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FkC3S4jjM9eI5TkJDPhBZ%2Fblobs%2F8FpN99efzafJjDkJuqgB%2Fimage.png&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=32a87f37&sv=2)

And there you are in your own custom world! Invite your friends, experiment and post stuff in Discord and on Twitter #mysomniaworld
